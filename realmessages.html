Sage Hill School

Students in Los Angeles are taking fashion into their own hands, promoting sustainability and upcyling. Sage Hill School has a club "Reimagined" which focuses on upcycling old clothing to reduce waste. Founder Anna Yang started the club after learning about upcycling and realizing the impact of discarded clothes in her own closet.

Shein Protests

Students in Los Angeles also are taking a stand against fast fashion brands like Shein and advocating for sustainable and ethical practices. On Earth Day 2024, these students gathered outside Shein's distribution center, protesting under the slogan "I'd rather be naked than wear Shein" to raise awareness about the environmental and social impacts of fast fashion brands like Shein.
